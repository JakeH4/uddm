GeoGuessr

I'm a little obsessed with GeoGuessr, a game that drops you somewhere in Google Street View and asks you to guess where on Earth you are. The closer your pin, the more points you score.

Clues I look for

  • Which side of the road traffic drives on
  • The language and letters on road signs
  • Road lines, bollards, and license plates
  • Vegetation, soil color, and climate

It looks simple, but after a few rounds you start noticing details you can never un-see.
